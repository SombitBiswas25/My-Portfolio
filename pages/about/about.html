About MySelf

Hello, I'm Sombit Biswas, a dynamic individual currently pursuing a Bachelor of Technology degree in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) while concurrently holding a diploma. My passion lies in web development, where I find joy in unraveling intricate coding concepts and making them accessible to others. As a dedicated tutor, I thrive on the challenge of transforming complex technical jargon into clear, understandable language. My goal is to empower my students by providing them with the tools and knowledge they need to succeed in the ever-evolving field of technology. I am deeply committed to staying abreast of the latest trends and advancements in web development. This dedication to continuous learning enables me to deliver up-to-date insights and practical skills to my students, equipping them to tackle real-world challenges with confidence. My approach to teaching is grounded in patience, empathy, and a genuine desire to see my students thrive. By fostering a supportive learning environment, I strive to instill in them not only a solid understanding of coding fundamentals but also a passion for lifelong learning and growth. In essence, I am more than just a tutor—I am a catalyst for transformation, helping aspiring developers like myself unlock their full potential and make meaningful contributions to the world of technology.
